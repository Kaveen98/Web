Ding Liren is the new world chess champion

Ding Liren reached the top of the chess world through a very unlikely journey.The 30-year-old from Zhejiang province bravely played for a win from what appeared to be a drawn position in the final of four quick games, despite never having the advantage during the three-week encounter at the St Regis Astana Hotel. That tenacity paid off handsomely when Ding became the first Chinese person to win the global chess championship when a surprised Nepomniachtchi resigned after 68 moves.
